Patternity by cyriux

Declare design intents in your code, and let tools automate your work

You think at design level, you deserve tools that work at design level. Don't you?

Enlarge your tools

  • Enforce dependencies between modules, layers or even between classes since patterns clearly define which participant should ignore each other
  • Generate relevant documentation because patterns know best what to show or hide in a given diagram
  • It's up to you to imagine further applications!
